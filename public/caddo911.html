Caddo911 is now Louisiana911

Caddo 911 calls, on a bigger Louisiana map.

Louisiana911 is built to be Louisiana's best, most up-to-date public 911 incident tracker. The project began with Caddo Parish and now brings multiple Louisiana public-safety feeds into one clear, searchable map.

Caddo Parish coverage

Live active events published by Caddo Parish 911, including Shreveport Police (SPD), Shreveport Fire (SFD), the Caddo Parish Sheriff's Office (CSO), Caddo Fire Districts (CFD), and publicly listed EMS incidents.

Open the Louisiana911 live map

Independent and unofficial. Louisiana911 is not affiliated with Caddo Parish 911, any responding agency, or the State of Louisiana. Public feed information can be delayed, incomplete, changed, or removed. For emergencies, call 911.

Louisiana911 also tracks available public incident information from Baton Rouge, Lafayette Parish, and New Orleans.
